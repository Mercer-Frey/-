Traditional "charity fair"

September 26, 2014 ... The collective of the school held a traditional "charity fair". This year, all the money earned was donated to wounded soldiers: a fighter of the Special Purpose Center for Counteracting Subversives and Terrorist Acts, Petr Yurievich Montin, diagnosed with a retinal bundle in the left eye due to cumulative poisoning in the amount of 6,297 UAH and Zagasail Sergey Vladimirovich, who received a craniocerebral injury - damage to the central nervous system of the brain in the amount of 5 127 UAH.

Preparing for the fair, the students of the school wrote letters and painted drawings to the participants of the fighting in the ATU with words of support and expectations for victory. Participants of the charity event were also representatives of the organization's chief organization LLC RPK VK SYSTEM ...

In the park Marjanovka, under the blue sky ...

Between the tall trees in the park, which is in the center of the village Marjanovka, stands a monument in the form of an armored reconnaissance and sentinel machine. In such a combat vehicle, young Soviet soldiers fought in the distant DRA, fulfilling their international duty. In a strange Muslim state among the mountains and sands fought with dushmans and guys from the city of Vasilkov and Vasilkovsky district. Twelve of them died a death of the brave, one is considered missing. Maryanov's mothers also held four sons for that terrible war. Thank God, everyone returned to their homes. Often, when they gathered together, the "Afghans" of the village commemorated their brethren, worshiped their memory and together with the village council put forward the idea of ​​perpetuating the memory of the residents of the region and the city that died in Afghanistan.

Valeriy Konstantinovich BUTESHOV, retired colonel, supported the concrete initiative in one of the first initiatives. The Ministry of Defense of Ukraine turned at the request of the former serviceman and saw an armored car - BRDM. For his own money, Colonel Buteshov restored the boyovumashinu and delivered to Maryanovka.
