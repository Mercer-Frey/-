Armored medical machine

АMM MT-LB S - It is designed for collection and removal of wounded from the battlefield and hotbeds of mass sanitary losses, with the provision of first aid ...

TACTICAL AND TECHNICAL CHARACTERISTICS

Based on the basic chassis MT-LB Sо following characteristics:

  • The length of the body - 6,454 m.
  • Width across the body - 2.86 m
  • Ground clearance - 0.4 m
  • The maximum speed along the highway is 60 km / h
  • Power reserve - 300 km.
CREW CREW
  • the commander of the car is the instructor-instructor
  • driver-mechanic
  • sanitary nurse (if necessary)
SPECIAL TOOLS
  • stretchers sanitary - 5 pcs.
  • equipment for loading and unloading the wounded
  • winch for retrieving the wounded from ravines, crevices and other hard-to-reach places
  • means of rendering first aid (-medicines;-dressings -covering harnesses
  • tires for immobilization; -high-oxygen inhaler KI-4.02
NUMBER OF PLACES FOR TRANSPORTATION OF WELL-BEING
  • in the position of lying on a stretcher - 4 (+1) person
  • in a sitting position - 8 people
  • combined - 4 sitting + 2 lying
  • The time of loading - unloading one wounded on stretchers is not more than 1 minute.
